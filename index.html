About Us

Our History

Founded in 1998 in the heart of Stockholm, our company began as a small, family-run construction firm focused on residential projects. Over the years, we’ve grown into a trusted name across Sweden, delivering commercial, industrial, and infrastructure projects with precision and reliability. With more than two decades of experience, we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern innovation.

Values

We are committed to quality, sustainability, and integrity in everything we do. From planning to execution, we prioritize safety, transparency, and client satisfaction. Our dedication to environmentally responsible building practices reflects our long-term vision for a better, greener future—one project at a time.

Licences and Registrations
  • Swedish Construction Industry Certification (Svensk Byggtjänst)
  • ISO 9001:2015
  • ISO 14001:2015
  • BAS-U & BAS-P Certified
  • ID06 Registered
  • Säker Vatten Certified
  • CE Marking Compliance

Our Services

Renovation

From modernizing outdated interiors to restoring historical properties, our renovation team brings craftsmanship and attention to detail to every project. We work closely with clients to transform spaces while preserving character, improving energy efficiency, and adding long-term value.

Demolition

Safe, efficient, and precise—our demolition services are tailored for both small-scale removals and large structural takedowns. We handle every step, from planning and permitting to site clearance, with a strong focus on safety and environmental responsibility.

Property Development

We develop residential, commercial, and mixed-use properties from concept to completion. With a focus on sustainable design, innovative planning, and community integration, we build spaces that meet today’s needs while anticipating tomorrow’s growth.
